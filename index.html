Grandma's Secret Apple Pie

This apple pie recipe has been in my family for generations and never fail to delight

Ingridents

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6 medium-seized apples , peeled and sliced

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375F (190C)
  2. In a bowl , mix the sugar and cinnamon
  3. Toss the apples in the sugar-cinnamon mixture
